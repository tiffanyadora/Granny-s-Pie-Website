OUR STORY

Since 1952, Granny's Pies has been serving up slices of happiness using time-tested family recipes and the finest ingredients. It all began when Granny Rose Thompson started baking pies from her home kitchen in downtown Nashville and sharing her creations with neighbors and friends. What started as a humble small business, with Rose selling pies from her front porch, quickly became the talk of the town. Her secret? A blend of patience, good ol' ingredients, and the belief that every pie should be baked with love.
